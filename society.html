About The Light

The Light Educational And Research Noble (LEARN) Welfare Society Basmat.

Objectives of The Light Educational And Research Noble Welfare Society:

1. To Promote Quality Education

To establish and support educational institutions that provide accessible, inclusive, and quality education from primary to higher levels, with special emphasis on underprivileged and marginalized communities.

2. To Encourage Research and Innovation

To foster a culture of research and innovation in various fields including science, technology, humanities, and social sciences, contributing to national and community development.

3. To Provide Skill Development and Vocational Training

To organize skill-building, technical, and vocational training programs for youth and women to enhance their employability and promote self-reliance.

4. To Uplift Socio-Economic Conditions

To implement welfare projects aimed at poverty alleviation, women empowerment, livelihood promotion, and community health and hygiene awareness.

5. To Promote Moral and Ethical Values

To inculcate moral, ethical, and human values in youth through educational programs, seminars, and cultural events aligned with national integrity and spiritual harmony.

6. To Support Orphans and Needy Students

To provide scholarships, educational materials, and other assistance to orphans, financially weak students, and those in rural or backward areas.

7. To Encourage Environmental Awareness and Action

To undertake programs related to environmental protection, tree plantation, clean energy awareness, and sustainability education.

8. To Collaborate for Development

To collaborate with like-minded NGOs, academic institutions, government agencies, and international bodies to strengthen educational and welfare initiatives.

9. To Publish and Disseminate Knowledge

To publish journals, books, newsletters, and digital content aimed at spreading knowledge, promoting research, and encouraging lifelong learning.

10. To Serve as a Resource Center

To function as a research and resource hub for educationists, social workers, and policy-makers engaged in community upliftment and educational reform.
